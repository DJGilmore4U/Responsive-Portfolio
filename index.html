How It All Works

We will set up a preliminary phone call to discuss the particulars & then set up a meeting so that I can demo what your product could look like. We will create a timeline & set milestones to give you an idea of how the project is expected to progress.

GOALS BRAINSTORM

I'll study your concept(s) from every angle & conduct ample research so that I can get a solid foundation to begin imagining the feel & culture that will benefit you most.

DESIGN DEVELOPMENT

Page layouts, review, & approval cycle are addressed. Then content writing & the assembly process followed by the coding stage where our idea begin to take on life!

TESTING LAUNCH

Here we will thoroughly test the finished product prior to deployment. Review of action items discussed in the first stage & finally the launch of your new product!
